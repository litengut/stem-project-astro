# McLaren

## History

McLaren is one of F1's great names, built on world titles, technical innovation, and some of the sport's most memorable driver lineups.

## 2026 Current Situation

McLaren is third on the current table with 18 points. The team has flashes of speed, but needs stronger weekend-to-weekend consistency to challenge the top two teams.

## Team Identity

McLaren's modern approach is data-driven and aggressive in development. The team tends to optimize quickly once it identifies the car's strongest operating window.

## What To Watch

The Norris-Piastri pairing remains one of the strongest on the grid, so any major upgrade step could quickly move McLaren back into regular podium contention.
