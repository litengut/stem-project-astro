# Mercedes

## History

Mercedes has one of Formula 1's deepest legacies, from its 1950s Silver Arrows era to its modern factory return in 2010. The team built one of the most dominant stretches in the hybrid era and became a benchmark for engineering discipline.

## 2026 Current Situation

Mercedes leads the current 2026 team standings snapshot with 98 points. George Russell and Kimi Antonelli are delivering a strong mix of consistency and speed, which gives the team momentum in both titles.

## Team Identity

Mercedes is known for process, precision, and race execution. Their weekends often look calm from the outside, but they are built on aggressive simulation work and very detailed setup planning.

## What To Watch

Watch how Mercedes balances short-term race wins versus long-term development through the season. If they keep both drivers scoring heavily, they stay in full control of the championship fight.
